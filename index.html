Escape the School. Don’t get caught.

ENG1 Group 7 is a school-themed maze escape game. You have five minutes to find a route out, trigger helpful classroom “events,” and avoid the teacher’s patrol.

Your final score is based on escape time and interactions with positive, negative, and hidden events.
